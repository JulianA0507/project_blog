My Catholic Faith

As a Chaldean Catholic, my faith is an integral part of my life. From serving in the church to attending weekly adoration, my spiritual journey is a source of strength, guidance, and community.

Serving in the Church

I have been serving in the Church for a year and a half now, and I absolutely love what I do. I read and sing, I am serving the Lord, and I am very thankful for this side of my life.

Weekly Adoration

I go to the beautiful adoration chapel in Mother of God every week. I pray for about 15-30 minutes at a time, and it is my peaceful time.

Chaldean Catholic

I am a Chaldean Catholic and Catholicism is something that I was born into. At first, I didn't really care for religion, and as I grew older, I came to know God and serve Him. I am a proud Chaldean Catholic.
